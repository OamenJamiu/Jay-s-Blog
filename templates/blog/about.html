My Names are Muhammad-Jamiu Oamen, Popularly Known as Jamiu Oamen. A citizen of Nigeria, born in Nigeria and took all Basic Education in Nigeria. I finished from a sound Secondary School Popularly Know as (NSSS), NEPA STAFF SECONDARY SCHOOOL in full. Located at Egbin, Ijede, Ikorodu. I took both my Primary And Secondary School Education there. also known as Basic School for countries that Don't use Primary And Secondary Schooling as their Naming System.
Leaving Secondary School was A great achievement for me, Been in a great Set in That Year. I knew by all faith that My calling Was/Is Aeronautical And Astronautical Engineering , But Going into that field without a high standard or above average Idea on Programming Languages Or ICT itself won't sound Good.
The year I finished Basic/Secondary School, I was meant to further myself in just Aeronautical And Astronautical Engineering but things didn't work out as it was meant to. Here in Nigeria One of the worst thing you won't want to experience is finishing Secondary/Basic school and not getting into University. You do think you are going to enjoy things around for a while. but not, its different here. This is the moment when you get to know life is not easy, because this time, This is when you will do all kinds of work at home you never thought you will do. Jamb is an organization in Nigeria that gives you access into University, So it's a must situation, You must do it for want to enter State or Federal University here. Jamb Simply means JOINT ADMISSION AND MATRICULATION BOARD nothing I could do than stay at home.
My wanted course was very rear here in Nigeria just two(2) Universities I know do it and One takes just OND, No Long story, I couldn't enter school that year. Staying at Home was getting very Obvious, So I told my Parents I couldn't just stay at home a whole year. I meant 365days at home, It turned out my Parents also were thinking about me being at home for a whole year, So the asked me what I was willing to learn and told them and that's How my Software Engineering Course started.

Getting into A private Institutions here was easy, all you needed was to have the capital and you are in. We went to the place together during his free time and My carrier at NiiT started. Own And ran by an Indian, So it's a foreign organization which was also developed here In NIGERIA. My Journey at NiiT started, First Three Months wasn't That easy, But in No Time, I was fine with it. Studied Every package Of Microsoft Office, From Word to Outlook also took Examinations on it, Including Networking. It was compulsory to take all those packages going into Software Engineering at NiiT. It was a must issue to also have to study One database Management Package, And the One done at NiiT at my time was (MS-SQL)MicroSoft SQL. Those listed packages had to be studied by every Software Engineering Student at (NiiT)NATIONAL INSTITUTION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGYY before going fully Into programming and In this case, it's PYTHON. After Examination in Previous Packages, I then started My Journey As A PROGRAMMER IN PYTHON.
It has been a nice Journey So Far,not so smooth due to endless time given to DEBUG than the actually PROGRAMMING. All Things being Good, I am focused On Web Designing and I use A very Nice FrameWork with Python Which is DJANGO. I am a PYTHON PROGRAMMER Now
